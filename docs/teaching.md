# Teaching and Mentoring

## Teachning and mentoring Activities

- **Final Year Projects (FYP)** — Regularly supervise diploma students on industry-relevant projects in battery management systems, fuel cell control, and IoT-enabled energy systems at Temasek Polytechnic
- **International Exchange Student Mentoring** — Coordinate and supervise **undergraduate** and **postgraduate** exchange students from Wuhan University of Technology, Southwest Jiaotong University, and Chongqing University
- **Continuing Education & Training (CET)** — Deliver professional training programmes on: 
    - Industry Best Practices in Compliance with International Safety Standards for Safe Installation, Operation & Maintenance of Energy Storage Systems (SP Group)
    - Introduction to Energy Storage Systems (SP Group)
    - Fuel cell stack and control systems (Industry partners)    
    - Lithium Battery Pack Upgrade & Maintenance Training for Extended Drone Flight Range (Power & Energy Engineering Centre, HQ MES/SAF)
        - Module 1: Fundamentals of Battery Management Systems (BMS) and Drone Power Systems
        - Module 2: Hands-on practical training for army personnel to assemble and test lithium battery packs for drone applications
