# Collaborations

## International Technology Partnerships

### Chongqing Innovation Fuel Cell Technology (CQFC)

**Role:** Technical Lead  
**Established:** April 2020  
**Investment:** RMB 45 million

A **Singapore-China joint venture** between Temasek Polytechnic and Chinese partners focused on the **commercialisation of fuel cell control systems**. As Technical Lead, I direct the R&D and technology transfer for PEM fuel cell control systems targeting the Chinese market.

---

### Wuhan University of Technology

**Role:** Project Coordinator & Initiator  
**Formalised:** MoU signed June 2024

Initiated and coordinated a **research collaboration and student exchange programme** between Temasek Polytechnic and Wuhan University of Technology. The partnership covers collaborative research in clean energy systems and provides opportunities for undergraduate and postgraduate student exchanges.

---

### Southwest Jiaotong University

Long-standing academic collaboration on clean energy research, student exchange, and joint supervision of research projects in battery management and fuel cell systems.

---

### Chongqing University

Research collaboration on fuel cell technology, power electronics, and energy system optimisation, with active student exchange programmes.
