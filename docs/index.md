## Research Focus

My work centres on translating laboratory innovations into commercially deployed clean energy products. Key research domains include:

- **Lithium battery management** — modelling, state estimation (SOC/SOH), fault diagnosis, and smart BMS
- **Fuel cell control technology** — characteristics, control strategies, thermal management, and multi-stack hybridisation
- **Energy hybridisation and management** — optimisation for ESS, electric vehicles, marine vessels, UAVs, and materials handling equipment
- **Smart energy IoT** — embedded systems, wireless BMS, cloud-based diagnostics, and edge computing
