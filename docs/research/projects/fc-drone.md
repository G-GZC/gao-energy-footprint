# Long-Endurance: Singapore's First Fuel Cell-Powered Drone

<span class="badge pi">PI</span> <span class="badge">2019–2023</span> <span class="badge">SGD 1.2M</span> <span class="badge">NRF CRP</span>

## Overview

This project delivered **Singapore's first hydrogen fuel cell-powered long-endurance drone** (25 kg class) with AI-enabled inspection capabilities. Funded by the National Research Foundation (NRF) under the Competitive Research Programme (CRP), the platform achieved over **2 hours of flight endurance** — a national first for fuel cell-powered UAVs.

The drone was successfully deployed in **March 2023** for smart water pipeline inspection for the **Public Utilities Board (PUB)**, demonstrating the viability of hydrogen-powered UAV systems for infrastructure inspection.

## Key Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Fuel cell type | PEM hydrogen fuel cell |
| Aircraft class | 25 kg multirotor UAV |
| Flight endurance | > 2 hours |
| Application | Smart infrastructure inspection |
| AI capabilities | Automated defect detection and analytics |

<!-- --- -->

## Technical Highlights

- **PEM fuel cell power system** — custom-designed for lightweight UAV integration with optimised thermal management
- **Hybrid power architecture** — fuel cell + battery hybridisation for peak power demands during take-off and manoeuvring
- **AI-enabled inspection** — onboard analytics for automated pipeline defect detection
- **Custom installation structure** — patented fuel cell and hydrogen tank mounting design for UAV (Chinese patent granted)

<!-- --- -->

## Impact

- **National first** — Singapore's first fuel cell-powered long-endurance drone
- Successfully deployed for PUB smart water pipeline inspection
- Demonstrated viability of hydrogen-powered UAV systems for large-scale infrastructure inspection
- Contributed to a granted patent on fuel cell UAV installation structure
